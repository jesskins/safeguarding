Safeguarding Stories

Read stories from individuals whose lives have been positively impacted by safeguarding efforts.

Annie's Story

Annie was a victim of domestic abuse. After reporting the abuse to the police and leaving her partner, she received practical, emotional, and financial support. This assistance helped her make positive changes in her life and that of her son.

Richard's Story

Richard left his home country of Poland for a job in the UK, but he soon found himself trapped in a nightmare situation. Safeguarding measures helped rescue him from human trafficking and provided him with the support he needed to recover.

Sam's Story

Andrea was supported through safeguarding policies after experiencing domestic abuse. The intervention helped her find safety and rebuild her life with the necessary resources and support.

Allan's Story

Allan, a child, was protected from abuse thanks to safeguarding policies in his school. The timely intervention and support from authorities ensured her safety and well-being.
